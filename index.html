About Ghandi Badela

I am an admitted advocate of the High Court of South Africa since 2011 and also a member of the Johannesburg Bar since 2012. Prior to being called to the Bar I worked for local and international companies(SAB, PEU and SAAB Grintek) at senior executive and strategic level for over 16 years.

I have also been a non-executive director of various State owned Entities including City Power, Denel, CSIR. Accordingly, I have experience in both the private and public sectors.

I hold Masters Degrees in Electromechanical Engineering, Engineering Management and Packaging Technology. Further, I obtained degrees in Bachelor of Laws, LLB, Business Administration(MBA) and Master of Laws, LLM, in intellectual Property. I have completed various courses in Domestic and international dispute resolution (mediation and arbitration) in Labour, Commercial, Construction and Engineering fields.

I am a director of the Arbitration Foundation of Southren Africa "AFSA". I am also a member of the Guiding Committee of China Africa Joint Arbitration Centre, "CAJAC". I am in the panel of arbitrators of Arbitration Foundation of Southren Africa(Commercial and construction arbitration), Association of Arbitrators Southren Africa (Construction Arbitration) and Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, UK. I am also a member of the International Institute of Space Law.

I was member of the BRICS Legal Forum Steering committee. I am serving as a Johannesburg Bar Council member for fourth consecutive period since 2017 to date.

I am a member of the Duma Nokwe and AFSA Group in Johannesburg, with my chambers at Fountain Chambers, Sandown Village Office Park, Corner Gwen Lane and 86 Maude Street, Sandton, Republic of South Africa.I am a member of the Polokwane Society of Advocates.
